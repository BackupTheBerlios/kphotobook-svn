FAQ KPhotoBook
How can I report bugs in KPhotoBook?
There is a development Mailinglist you can subscribe to discuss bugs. Additionally you can use the tracker on the project homepage at berlios.de. Please do not send an email concerning bugs to the developer directly.
How can I report issues within this homepage?
Please send an email to the maintainer of the homepage. Email to Tom.
